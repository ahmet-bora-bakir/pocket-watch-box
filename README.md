# pocket-watch-case#
A custom-designed storage and protection solution for pocket watches, featuring two different lid mechanisms to suit your preference.

---

## 🎯 Purpose
The objective of this project is to provide a safe, dust-free, and impact-resistant environment for vintage or modern pocket watches. The interior dimensions are optimized to prevent the watch from rattling while ensuring easy access.

---

## 📦 Design Versions
This project includes two distinct versions of the housing:

1.  **Sliding Lid Version:** Features a precision-fit sliding mechanism. Ideal for a sleek look and secure closure without needing extra hardware.
2.  **Standard (Non-Sliding) Version:** A traditional lift-off or snap-fit lid design for quick and easy access to your timepiece.

---

## 🛠 Technical Specifications & Assembly
* **Source Files:** The original **FreeCAD (.FCStd)** files are provided, allowing you to scale the case dimensions to fit specific watch diameters (e.g., 45mm, 50mm, etc.).
* **Post-Processing (Crucial):** * For the **Sliding Version**, light **sanding** on the rails is highly recommended to ensure a smooth, fluid sliding motion. 
    * Applying a small amount of paraffin wax or dry lubricant to the rails can further improve the mechanism.
* **Interior Lining:** It is recommended to place a thin layer of **felt or soft fabric** inside the case to provide extra cushioning and prevent scratches on the watch's polished surfaces.

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes
* **Tolerances:** Depending on your printer's calibration, the sliding lid may be tight initially. Do not force it; sand the edges until the fit is perfect.
* **Safety:** Ensure the case is free of any 3D printing "strings" or debris before placing your watch inside.
